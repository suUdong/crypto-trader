# Strategy Parameter Tuning Progress

## Root Cause Analysis
- Composite: momentum >= 0.02 AND near_lower_band AND RSI 25-45 are contradictory
- Momentum: momentum >= 0.02 AND RSI 25-45 conflict (strong momentum => high RSI)
- Mean Reversion: 2-sigma lower band is too rare (~5% of candles)
- BacktestEngine hardcoded to CompositeStrategy type hint — can't backtest other strategies
- All strategies share one StrategyConfig, so tuning affects all three
